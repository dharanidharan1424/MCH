Their Story

From a Lime Soda to
180+ Stores.

"We didn't set out to build a chain. We set out to make one honest cup of coffee accessible to every Indian." Prasanna Venkatesh, Co-Founder
Heroes of Chennai 2018 Best F&B Chain, Chennai

It began with a chance meeting in 2008, not over coffee, but over a lime soda. Prasanna Venkatesh, then working as a medical representative and hailing from Madurai, struck up a conversation with M Kumaaravelan, who was managing a hotel in the bustling market hub of Koyambedu, Chennai. What started as a shared glass turned into a shared vision: to bring authentic, high-quality South Indian filter coffee to urban India at everyday prices.

Prasanna had always carried entrepreneurial instincts. During his college years at Sourashtra College, Madurai, he ran a small tiffin shop part-time, his first lesson in customer service, speed, and food quality. Kumaaravelan brought the institutional weight: over 25 years in the hospitality industry, with deep expertise in kitchen operations, vendor management, and large-scale service delivery. Together, they were the perfect complement.

In 2010, Madras Coffee House was born. The brand was built around a simple but powerful idea: a coffee-only express service model that put the cup first. Drawing inspiration from the traditional South Indian darshini style, with self-service, standing tables and no frills, they created a kiosk format that was fast, clean, and consistent. Every outlet was designed to be compact (300 to 600 sq ft), high-throughput, and instantly recognisable through the brand's bold red-and-white visual identity.

Growth was steady and deliberate. Rather than dining restaurants, MCH focused on office parks, metro corridors, and transit hubs, spaces where time-pressed professionals needed quality coffee without the cafe wait. The model worked: the brand expanded beyond Chennai to Gurgaon, Noida, Mumbai and other metros, building a network of over 85 outlets across India. In 2018, the brand received national recognition when it was named Best Food and Beverage Chain in Chennai at the Heroes of Chennai Awards.

Today, Madras Coffee House is a trusted household name with an ambitious roadmap ahead. The co-founders are driving expansion into Tier-2 cities and international markets, while also developing a new concept called Chaya Maaya, a dedicated tea outlet brand, to extend their philosophy of affordable, authentic South Indian beverages to new audiences across India and beyond.
